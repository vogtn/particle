    Safety Pin
    • featured tech: react, react-router, oauth(passport), nodejs

    Safety is a simple concept that every individual should be able to enjoy. Safety Pin queries the Seattle police department for recent crime data, and then places "pins" on a googlemap representing the location of the respective crimes. The location is also centralized based on the user's location.
